ABOUT US

  1. The ASCA association

    2. The Angels Sportive and Cultural Association (ASCA) was created in late 2006 at the University of Yaounde II, Soa, in Cameroon, with the mission of sourcing young talents in various fields such as music, cinema, painting, theatre etc. According to its new operational needs, ASCA evolved into two split entities: Angels Sport Academy (ASA) and Angels Creative Arts (ACA).

  2. The ACA project

    3. The Angels Creative Arts (ACA) project was born within the scope of ASCA, thanks to a fruitful collaboration with The African Frequency project who provided its immense help on many technical aspects in respects of the ASCA creative activities.

  3. ACA's goals and mission:

    4. The main goal of ACA is to promote the educational improvement of various talents already affiliated with the association as well as many others willing to improve their creative skills in a valuable manner, through a large number of projects uselful for our developping society.

ACA current missions:

Angels Basketball Club

ACA will assist Angels Basketball Club for the recording and broadcasting of matches and other major events. Our objective is to connect our audience to our vibrant activities.

Social Media Engagement

The Association is planning to increase its visibily on various social media platforms for cultural and educative purposes.

Connecting Universities

ACA will also provide a helful platform to help talents willing to implement their creative ideas into valuable developmemt projects.
