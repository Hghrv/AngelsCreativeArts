A quick overview of our recent activities at ACA in 2023:

Music

The music department consist of: Education, self-development and artistic releases. The educational aspect covers the advanced jazz music theory and practical concepts, while the self-development and releases can provide evaluation and guidance towards royalties management.

Arts and Culture

The Association is aiming towards the production of cultural and educative arts and craft of high quality content.

Cinema

Our special team has a lot of Audio-Visual projects in view, from theatral training to video production and 4D animations.

Sports and Martial Arts

ACA is a department of the broader ASCA association which was established in 2004 in Cameroon with the support of the University of Yaounde II, Soa. The ASCA innitially launched a local Basketball Club called ANGELS.
