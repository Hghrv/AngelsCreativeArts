ACA educative programs

The Association is aiming at the production of cultural and educative arts and craft with high quality content. ACA educational activities include:

- Writing a script for short video

- Acting and theatre ateliers for live performance

- Short audio-video projects

Our special team has a lot of Audio-Visual projects in view, from theatral training to video production and 4D animations.

ACA is a department of the broader ASCA association which was established in 2004 in Cameroon with the support of the University of Yaounde II, Soa. The ASCA innitially launched a local Basketball Club called ANGELS.
